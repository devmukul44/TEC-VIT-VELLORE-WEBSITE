This Club has been found with a vision of inculcating the skills into the students related to Electronics and to motivate them to disseminate the knowledge to their surroundings. We provide an education that result in understanding and the spread of knowledge. The platform provided by this club, is meant to enrich the practical experience of the students, and to prompt them with a suitable way of doing systematic investigation to establish facts. They are trained here to understand the correct attempt to find out the results and facts in a systematic and scientific manner. We as a TEAM have a mission to apprise the students, the behavior that befit or comport a young engineer.
