Introduction

Keeping in view the response for Electronics 101 conducted the previous year, TEC started this fall sem with version 2.0 of the same event. Electronics 101 V2.0 was conducted on 26th july 2013 in TT 238 from 6pm onwards. It started with an introduction to the club and it’s working procedure by Sarthak Dubey. The event included introduction to basic electronic components like resistors ( colour coding) ,capacitors(value reading), inductors, diodes, Led’s, transistors etc.. It was followed by a free hands –on session of making simple ”LED- brightness control” circuit and all the materials were provided to them by TEC and SENSE-VIT




Feedback

The feedback in totality was very appreciating for the club. Students liked the session and worked very enthusiastically throughout the event. The experience of making their first circuit on a breadboard was very exciting to them.
