Introduction

The first event organized by TEC in the year 2013, wherein students were introduced to the working and applications of 555 timers. The event was held on 1st February 2013 at TT238 from 6:00 to 7:30pm.This session was a hands on session. Every student who attended this session got an opportunity to practically work with a 555 timer.


Operation

A 555 timer consists of 25 transistors, 2 diodes and 15 resistors on a silicon chip installed in a DIP-8 package. Using this timer an LED was made to blink with a fixed time constant. The timer was operated in the astable mode.



Feedback

The feedback was in favor of the club. Students were satisfied with the knowledge that they had gained. Students liked the session and worked very enthusiastically throughout the event.. Most of the participants were from ECE branch so they found the idea of hands-on session very helpful in their academic front also.
