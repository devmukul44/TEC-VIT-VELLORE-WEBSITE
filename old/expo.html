Introduction

TEC, the electronics club organized an exhibition on 8th March, 2013, where the club members and external participants put on display, numerous projects, each using different principles. The event started at 6 p.m., in TT-238, with 85 participants attending the exhibition. The event was attended by a mixed audience of 1st years, 2nd years and faculties from the SENSE department of VIT.

Feedback

The exhibition received an appreciable feedback. Students and the faculties were really impressed by the projects that were put up. The seniors mentored their junior exceptionally well in putting up such an exceptional exhibition.
