Electronics 101 V2.0 :


Event Date : 02/08/2013, 03/08/2013
Time : 6 pm- 7.30pm
Venue : TT205 and TT238
Organizer : The Electronics Club(TEC)
Participant Count : 50
Speaker(s) Volunteers :Vibhutesh Kumar Singh, Sonali Adnani, Saumya Shekhar, Dheeraj Mor and Vinay Shah.



Introduction


The session began with an introduction to the club by the President.

As the name implies, the purpose of the event was to introduce participants to the basic elements of electronics. The purpose of the theory session was to inform students about components like capacitors, resistors diodes, rectifiers etc. their characteristics, types and applications. This was followed by a hands-on experience for the same and other electronic instruments like ammeter, voltmeter, multimeter, voltage regulator, function generator, CRO etc. through a lab session. About 50 participants attended the events. They were quite satisfied with the overall experience and expressed their wish for more of such theory + lab events in the future to clear their basics.
