Introduction

A three round quiz based on electronics .
Round 1- GENERAL QUIZ:
10-15 easy to medium level questions related to electronics were asked in MCQ format and one-fourth of the participants were forwarded to next round. .
Round 2- BRICK BREAKER
A mysterious picture related to electronics was hidden behind 9 bricks. Participants were asked to break bricks numbered from 1 to 9.Teams which identified the picture correctly after breaking minimum no. of bricks gained maximum no. of points. And in the next stage 6 pictures somehow related to the disclosed picture, participants are asked to find out the relation between them . based on the scores few top teams were selected for the last round.
Round 3- SPIN A WHEEL
A wheel was installed in front of the participants. Wheel was designed in such a way that it would be set in motion as soon as team members say 'START' and it would stopped on team's command of 'STOP'. Wheel contained many options such as videos, speech or audio, projects related to electronics and many more. Teams were asked to answer the questions related to the option which they have encountered on the wheel. The team with highest number of points a t the end of third round was declared winner.
